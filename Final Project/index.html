People were looking themselves in the mirror and wondering, Why are my condiment options so boring?! That day scared the people at Evan&Kevin to come up with a solution, that solution is THE SAUCE BOSS.

THE SAUCE BOSS offers all the things that you never thought could be true!! with our patented screw conveyor dispensary technology we make whatever sauce you could think of. With our variety of flavors and spices, you will never get bored.

There are 4 ways we plan to sell our product. A large scale version for chain fast food resturaunts. These Will have all of the different spices and ingredients that we have to offer. Then there will be a smaller, but still industrial model for mom and pop. This option is cheaper and you can specialize the spices that you want to put in your sauce. Then there is the at home sauce boss for the avid fan which is slightly snaller than the industrial model, but runs through an Ipad. Then we also plan to sell our sauces in bottles ranging from 12 to 32 ounces.

Now that people can decide which sauce that they use there will be no need for war anymore. The world will shortly become a utopia. So you are welcome, but we are kind of a big deal.
