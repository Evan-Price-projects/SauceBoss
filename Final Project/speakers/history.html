It all started in the year 2017 when founders Evan and Kevin had a vision that they were going to change the world. It all starts with not knowing which condiment they wanted to dip their fries in. So they got a plate and put multiple condiments on the plate. The condiments combined and the taste improved like the paints on an art canvas combining to make deeper colors. The rest is history.
